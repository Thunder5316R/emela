Return, Refund &
Replacement Policy

Emela E-commerce Company Limited

This policy follows the Bangladesh consumer law and global e-commerce standards.

Return Eligibility

Customers may request a return within 7 days of:

  • The product is defective or damaged.
  • The wrong product was delivered.
  • The product significantly differs from its description.

Non-Returnable Items

Items excluded unless defective:

  • Perishable goods (food, groceries, eggs).
  • Personal hygiene items once opened.
  • Digital products after access.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unused & in original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase is required.
  • Emela reserves the right to inspect items.

Refund Policy (BD Compliant)

  • Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 working days.
  • Refunds are issued through original payment method.
  • Full refund if Emela fails to deliver within timeline.

Replacement Policy

  • Customers may choose a replacement instead of a refund.
  • Subject to product availability.
  • If unavailable, a full refund will be issued.

Fraud Prevention

Refunds may be denied in cases of:

  • False claims or repeated abuse.
  • Item misuse or damage after delivery.

Customer Support

Submit requests with:

  • Order ID & Description of issue.
  • Product photos or videos.
