Safety Center

Emela Company Limited

Emela Company Limited prioritizes the safety of its customers, sellers, and partners. Our Safety Center provides guidelines, tools, and rules to help ensure a secure experience.

1. Purpose of the Safety Center

Promote safe buying and selling practices

Prevent fraud, abuse, and misuse of the platform

Provide clear procedures for reporting concerns

Support compliance with laws and regulations

2. Account Safety

Use strong and unique passwords

Keep login credentials private

Log out from shared or public devices

Emela does not support transactions outside the platform.

3. Transaction Safety

Complete all payments through Emela

Avoid sharing financial info with unknown parties

Be cautious of unusually cheap or urgent offers

Safety first, trust your instincts.

4. User Responsibilities

Follow Policies

Stay updated with community standards.

Act Honestly

Be responsible in every interaction.

Report Issues

Help us keep the platform safe.
